Crystal Collector Game

How to play:

  1. When the game starts you will be given a number that you want your score to equal.
  2. Below you will find four different crystals:
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
    • Blue

  3. When you click on a crystal, points will be added to your score.
  4. Each crystal will have a different value that is unknown until you click on it.
  5. To win you need to match "Your Score" to the "Score to Reach"
  6. You will lose the game if you go over the "Score to Reach"
  7. When the game is restarted, each crystal will have a new random value.
